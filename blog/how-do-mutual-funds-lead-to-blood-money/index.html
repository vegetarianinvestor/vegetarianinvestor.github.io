How do mutual funds lead to blood money

  • June 15, 2021
  • Posted by Amit
  • Mutual Funds, Blood Money

Mutual funds are a type of investment scheme that pools together investors money and invest it in different types of financial instruments such as stocks, bonds, government securities, gold, etc. Only when you see the holdings inside your mutual fund you would know the companies your money is invested in.

How do fund managers decide where to invest

The only factor taken into consideration by the fund managers to decide where to invest are the financial indicators like PE ratio, intrinsic value, annual reports etc. The companies in which the fund manager foresees profits, that’s where the funds are pushed. How does the company manufacture the product they sell, is never reviewed.

Sectoral Mutual Funds

Mutual funds do have a category called “Sectoral Mutual Funds” which are equity schemes that invest in a specific sector of the economy for example Banking, Energy, Technology, Infrastructure etc. These too do not stick to their chosen mandate for investment which in itself could be worded cleverly by using the words “investing predominantly” in companies of the particular sector. In addition to this, a disclaimer in their offer document could clearly state that there is no assurance that the investment objective of the scheme will be carried out. And last but not least, due to wide diversification, companies they invest in for the sectorial mutual fund could easily get linked directly or indirectly to some other sector.

The bottomline is that as an investor we do not have any control over which company gets funded through our money. Our money could be invested in companies under animal husbandary, poultry, pharmaceutical, leather industries etc causing a large number of animals getting killed. This is how our money gets converted to blood money

Move towards cruelty free investments

Vegetarian Investments tackle this problem by doing direct equity research and choosing only those companies that are cruelty free. The benefit is twofold - our investments remain green and cruelty free companies get promoted leading to a kinder world in future.
